3. Platform Description

CanadaFlowers.ca is an online flower and gift delivery platform that enables users to browse and purchase floral arrangements, plants, and gift baskets for occasions such as birthdays, anniversaries, and sympathy events. The platform partners with local florists across Canada to provide same-day or next-day delivery depending on order cut-off times. Users can personalize orders by selecting delivery dates and including custom gift messages.

4. Data Collected

  • Personal Information: Email address, recipient name, delivery address, phone number
  • Order Details: Products selected, delivery date, gift message, purchase history
  • Payment Information: Billing and payment details (handled by third parties)
  • Inferred Data: IP address, approximate location, device and browser type
  • Behavioural Data: Pages viewed, time on site, cart activity

6. Ethical Analysis

CanadaFlowers.ca collects sensitive personal data such as home addresses, phone numbers, and personalized gift messages, which may contain emotionally sensitive content. Behavioural tracking through cookies and analytics may also occur without users fully understanding how their data is collected, retained, or shared.

If mishandled or exposed, this data could result in identity theft, financial fraud, unwanted marketing, or exposure of private communications. Limited transparency around third-party data sharing further increases ethical risk.

7. Recommendations

  1. Data Minimization: Limit collection and retention to essential information only.
  2. Transparent Consent: Use clear, accessible language to explain data practices.
  3. Reduce Third-Party Sharing: Audit and limit external partners handling user data.
